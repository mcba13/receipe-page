Rougail Sausages

Impossible to visit the intense island without warming up your taste buds with a rougail sausage. A quintessential Creole dish, its hearty nature and balanced composition (meat, vegetables, starches) once delighted workers. Rice, beans, spices, and meats, a winning quartet that has withstood the test of time and made its way to our plates. Both spicy and mild, rich and humble, rougail sausage remains an invitation to share, in all simplicity.

Ingredients

  • 2 fresh sausages
  • 2 smoked sausages
  • 4 small chili peppers
  • 4 tomatoes
  • 2 onions
  • Ginger
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oil

Instructions

  1. Cut the tomatoes into small pieces
  2. Cut the onions
  3. Cut the ginger
  4. Boil the sausages for 10 minutes
  5. Cut the sausages
  6. Put the chili peppers in a mortar
  7. Add salt
  8. Pound
  9. Add the ginger
  10. Pound
  11. Heat oil
  12. Saut√© the sausage pieces
  13. Add the onions
  14. Add the spices
  15. Brown them
  16. Add the tomatoes, mix well
  17. Cover and simmer for 20 minutes
